How to Play

Crack safes. Stack rewards. Cash out before it's too late.

The Basics

Each level presents 3 to 7 safes. Only one triggers the alarm — the rest contain gold.

  • Pick a safe — drill it open to see what's inside.
  • Gold? You earn points and advance to the next level.
  • Alarm? Game over — you lose all your unbanked points.

Scoring & Multiplier

Fewer safes means higher base points but riskier odds. As you advance levels, a multiplier increases your prize — but so does the risk.

Cash Out

Press "KEEP REWARD" at any time to bank your points to the leaderboard. If you get greedy and lose, you walk away with nothing.

Wallet

Use CONNECT WALLET to link your main wallet address — your scores will appear under that address on the leaderboard.
